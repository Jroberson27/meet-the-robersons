Our Story

Real family.
Real adventures.

We're Jordan & Ashly, parents to six amazing kids and Orlando locals who literally live minutes from Universal Orlando Resort and Walt Disney World. Theme parks aren't a vacation for us. They're our backyard.

What started as capturing our family's trips turned into a full-time mission: helping real families navigate theme parks, plan smarter vacations, and make memories that last. From Epic Universe opening day to Fort Wilderness at Christmas. If it's worth doing, we're documenting it.

No fluff. No fake reactions. Just a family of 8 who loves every minute of this life and wants to bring you along for the ride.
